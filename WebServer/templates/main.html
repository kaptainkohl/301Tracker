About

The Nintendo 64 Rareware Collectathon Race, an idea originally conspired between Konditioner and 2dos, has now now been fully established into a major event between the Rareware speedrunners and their communities. The Rareware platformer collectathon games are Banjo-Kazooie, Banjo-Tooie, and Donkey Kong 64.

There are three main race categories that are run with these games:

Any% - BK Any%, BT Any%, and DK64 Any%
Restricted Any% - BK Any% No RBA, BT Any% No DCW, and DK64 No Levels Early
301% - BK 100%, BT 100%, and DK64 101%

Currently, the 301% Race is run on an annual basis within the community. No other races have been planned at this time.
