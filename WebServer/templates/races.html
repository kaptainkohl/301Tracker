301% Race

The Rareware Collectathon 301% Race consists of completing Banjo-Kazooie 100%, Banjo-Tooie 100% and Donkey Kong 64 101% in one race back to back to back. This is the most popular category for the Rareware races and is currently being raced on an annual basis.


Currently Scheduled Races

January 28, 2018


Race Rules

- The race will be hosted on SpeedRunsLive
- The race must be done in BK-BT-DK64 order
- All official releases of the games are allowed
- Unofficial emulators are banned
- The race is done RTA style. Breaks shorter than an hour are allowed, but will count against your final time
- Breaks longer than an hour result in disqualification, per SpeedRunsLive rules
- Streaming is required
- All rules pertaining to the 3 categories must be followed (see respective speedrun.com pages for more info)
- Furnace Fun Moves is allowed for Banjo-Kazooie and can be set up before the race begins, per BK 100% rules
